The 7-Gate Gauntlet

Every strategy. Every time. No exceptions.
01
Dry run
A week of bars, verbose logs. Catches inverted signals, never-exits.
02
Single-period
2-year window, realistic costs. Equity curve resemble the source's claim?
03
Multi-regime
2017 calm · Mar–Jun 2020 · 2022 bear · 2023 AI bubble · 2024 chop.
04
Param sensitivity
Sweep ±50% on every knob. Big P&L swings = overfit.
05
Walk-forward
Optimize → trade T→T+6mo locked → re-optimize. Real-world simulation.
06
Cost stress
2× costs · 2× slippage. Especially load-bearing without L2.
07
Kill decision
Pass → Phase 6. Fail → archived with post-mortem. Never deleted.

Why now

Watching a research-only strategy fire on garbage in real time is a cheap way to learn it's bad before you spend weeks backtesting it. The dashboard becomes a research accelerator, not just a monitor.

The one rule

No new strategies or logic after entering live. Anything new goes back through Phase 5 → 6 first. This is the discipline that prevents a clean system from drifting into chaos.

iii.

Load-bearing constraints

These shape every design choice downstream. Don't quietly relax them.
No Level 2 data in v1

Polygon Developer tier doesn't include order-book depth. The v1 source's "watching it build" filter has to use a 1-min volume proxy. This is the project's biggest codeability risk; Gate 6 is designed to expose it.

PDT rule at < $25k

Intraday strategy + $1–2k starter capital collides with Pattern Day Trader limits. Must be resolved by end of Phase 6 — otherwise the bot can't execute its own playbook.

Universe = NASDAQ / QQQ + liquidity filter

Stocks only, point-in-time membership snapshotted monthly. Survivorship-free backtests demand it.

Cloud-portability seam

Only Phase 1 may require local files. From Phase 2 forward, everything runs against committed transcripts + a rebuildable SQLite. Remote / iOS sessions depend on this not breaking.

Public-repo redaction

Repo is public/MIT. Source brand, author, distinctive coined terms, and specific extracted entities never enter tracked files. Specifics live in the local DB and gitignored notes.

Backtest reports are immutable

Dated, never overwritten, never edited post-hoc. Hypothesis logged before the run. This is the single discipline that prevents overfitting-by-iteration.

If < 1 strategy survives Phase 5 after a full course dump, the wiki is the win.
Do not lower gates. Do not force marginal candidates through.
